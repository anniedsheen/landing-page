Fall Seasonal Selection

Toasted Marshmallow Latte: Double shot espresso with steamed milk and house-made toasted marshmallow syrup, topped with a soft cloud of foam and a charred mini marshmallow.

Yuzu Sparkling Tea: A gently sparkling oolong tea with a hint of citrusy yuzu and a whisper of brown sugar syrup. Bright, cozy, and a refreshing break from all things creamy.

Black Sesame Kouign Amann: A classic French pastry with a crisp caramelized shell and soft, flaky layers, filled with nutty black sesame paste.

Miso Caramel Crumb Cake: A buttery crumb cake swirled with miso caramel and topped with a toasted oat crumble. Sweet, savory, and deeply satisfying.
